Donate

At Boops & Snoots Rescue, every donation, big or small, makes a real difference in the lives of the animals we care for. Here's how you can help:

For Monetary Donations

Your financial support helps us provide food, medical care, and the resources needed to care for our animals. We're incredibly grateful for every donation, no matter how small. You can donate securely via our PayPal or JustGiving page.


Boops & Snoots Rescue T-Shirts

Support our rescue and look great doing it! Boops & Snoots Rescue t-shirts are available to buy only at the shelter for £15. Every purchase helps us care for our animals.

Available in children's sizes and adult XS to XL.

Come visit us and grab yours!

Food Donations

We always welcome donations of in-date, sealed pet food to ensure our animals are well-fed while they wait for their forever homes. You can drop off food donations directly at our centre during opening hours.

For Physical Donations

If you'd like to donate food, toys, bedding, or other items, please contact us to arrange a drop-off time. We're always in need of the following items:

  • Wet and dry pet food
  • Blankets and towels
  • Collars and leads
  • Scratching posts and toys
  • Cleaning supplies
  • Kitty litter and potty pads
  • Pet beds large and small

For more information on how to donate physical items, please contact us or visit our shelter during opening hours
