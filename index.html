Welcome to Boops & Snoots Rescue

We help pets find their forever homes

At Boops & Snoots Rescue, we're passionate about giving pets a second chance and finding them the loving homes they deserve. Based in the heart of North Wales, we are dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need. We also promote responsible pet ownership and support the well-being of our community through our events. We believe every animal deserves to be loved, cared for, and treated with kindness. Whether you're looking to adopt, volunteer, or donate, your support helps make a difference in the lives of countless animals. Join us in our mission to create a better world for animals and their humans, one snoot at a time!
