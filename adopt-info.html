If you'd like to meet some of our pets in person, we're happy to arrange a visit. Not all animals are housed in the kennels or cattery at all times, so it's best to get in touch beforehand. Our friendly team can help you set up a convenient appointment.

Why Adopt?

Adopting a pet doesn't just change their life
it changes yours too! Here's how:

  • Save a Life

    By adopting, you're giving an animal a second chance. Many of our pets come to us from challenging situations, and your decision to adopt means they'll get the loving home they deserve.

  • Make Room for Others in Need

    Every adoption creates space in our rescue, allowing us to help even more animals in need of care, safety, and love.

  • Enjoy the Rewards of Companionship

    Pets enrich our lives in countless ways:

    • Unconditional Love: The bond between you and your adopted pet is unbreakable.
    • Improved Wellbeing: Pets have been shown to reduce stress, combat loneliness, and even improve your health by encouraging exercise and routine.
    • Joyful Moments: From funny antics to heartwarming cuddles, pets bring endless happiness to your home.
  • Support Ethical Animal Care

    Adopting from Boops & Snoots Rescue ensures you're supporting responsible pet ownership. All our animals are:

    • Vaccinated
    • Microchipped
    • Spayed or Neutered
    • Health Checked
    • Behaviourally assessed to ensure they're matched with the perfect home - one that's safe and loving for both the animal and everyone in the family, including children.
  • Find a Perfect Match

    Our team is dedicated to helping you find the pet that fits your family, lifestyle, and energy level. Whether you're looking for a playful puppy, a calm older dog, or a cuddly cat, we'll work with you to make the best match.
