Adopt a New Best Friend

Find a Friend

At Boops & Snoots Rescue, we believe every pet deserves a loving home and a second chance. If you're looking to adopt, we're here to help you find the perfect furry companion to fit your family and lifestyle.


Browse Available Animals

We make it easy for you to find your new best friend. You can:

  • Follow us on social media: Check out regular updates, photos, and stories of our adoptable animals on Facebook and Instagram.

  • Contact us directly: Looking for a specific type of pet? Reach out using our contact form or call us and our team will help match you with the perfect companion.

  • Visit our shelter: Schedule an appointment to meet our animals in person and see who you fall in love with.

Plan Your Visit

If you'd like to meet some of our pets in person, we're happy to arrange a visit. Not all animals are housed in the kennels or cattery at all times, so it's best to get in touch beforehand. Our friendly team can help you set up a convenient appointment.
